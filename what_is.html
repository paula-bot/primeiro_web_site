What is HyperText

Hypertext is text that is not restricted to being linear.

Hypertext is a text that contains links to other texts. The term was coined by Ted Nelson around 1965 (see History).

HyperMedia is a term used for hypertext that is not limited to text: it can include graphics, video and sound, for example. Apparently, Ted Nelson was also the first to use that term.

Hypertext and HyperMedia are concepts, not products.
